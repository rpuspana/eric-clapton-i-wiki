Eric Clapton interactive wikipedia

Eric Patrick Clapton, (born 30 March 1945), is an English rock and blues guitarist, singer, and songwriter. Clapton has been referred to as one of the most important and influential guitarists of all time.

I wanted to pay tribute to his great muisc by creating this game, where you can test your knowledge about his music and music journey.

To play this game you need to :
- read the game rules.
- bring up your browser's console.
- click "Start game" to start playing.
